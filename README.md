# PalmX-Hub
Genomic resources and multi-omics platforms for oil palm breeding, stress resilience and sustainable tropical agriculture.
PalmX-Hub is an integrated genomic and multi-omics platform for accelerating oil palm (Elaeis guineensis) improvement. By combining haplotype-resolved pangenomics, population genomics, transcriptomics, proteomics, metabolomics and single-cell technologies, PalmX-Hub aims to bridge the gap between genome architecture and agronomically important traits — including oil yield and quality, cold tolerance for northward range expansion, and resilience to biotic and abiotic stresses in tropical environments. Our long-term goal is to provide the genomic infrastructure for precision breeding of high-yielding, climate-adaptive oil palm varieties that can sustain productivity on existing land while reducing pressure on tropical ecosystems.
